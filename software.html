TECHNICAL SKILLS

C

Low-level design excites me because you become very close to the hardware and can exploit significant performance improvements. I am very interested in constructing libraries and data structures at a low-level, as well as transforming low level constructs from assembly to C. I enjoy the concept of manual memory management and use it to my advantage, bringing significant performance improvements in the design of algorithms.
