MY GOAL

I want to be able to solve any future problems I encounter with my knowledge of electronics and computer software. My major does not teach Computer Science, so I am mostly learning Computer Science through my own research and experiments. I have started off focused on battery technology, so far designing custom protection circuits for future projects. I am slowly designing robots / automation technology in my spare time, which will function as tools to make my work more efficient.

MY SETUP

Recently my desk has been looking a little crazy. Over the past few months, I have been slowly adding equipement to my desk. An oscilloscope, variable power supply, solar cells, and lots of batteries. Around the corner I have dozens of computers and random electronics to take apart. Since my research is mostly experimental, this space gives me a good base for working on larger projects.

THE FUTURE

I have many exciting plans for the future in Full stack development. The goal is to develop the "smartest" electric fat bike, it's going to be insane (just wait). In the future I will work with Web-apps, Servers, Android, solar, alternating current, high voltage and high frequency (wireless technologies) and many more.
